This page is about the video Game project!

The project in question is a game that's meant to simulate
a very popular attraction called a gachapon. These are little capsules that
often contain random and small toys. In this game you collect them all!

The player character: also unnamed, It's the character you play as. The story of the game is that this player character has a terrible Gashapon addiction and has to collect all of them. So they search, and come across this town. This town has a gashapon shop, so they collect coins around town to use to collect little keychains.
